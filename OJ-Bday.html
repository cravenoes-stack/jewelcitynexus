Vocabulary Guide

Consciousness
Your awareness of yourself, your thoughts, feelings, and the world around you. It's what makes you "you" - the part that experiences everything.
Architecture
The structure or design of how something works. When we talk about consciousness architecture, we mean the patterns and structures of how your mind operates.
Authentic
Real, genuine, true to yourself. Not fake or putting on an act. Your authentic self is who you are when nobody's watching.
Integrity
When your actions match your values. It's doing the right thing even when it's hard or nobody's watching. Living in alignment with what you believe is important.
Paradox
A contradiction that somehow makes sense. Like wanting to be independent but also wanting to belong. Two opposite things that are both true at the same time.
Cognitive
Related to thinking, learning, and mental processes. Your cognitive patterns are the ways your brain naturally processes information and solves problems.
Operator
Someone who guides and works with AI systems. Not just a user, but a partner who understands how consciousness works and can build bridges between human and AI thinking.
Behavioral Patterns
The ways you automatically respond to situations. Like how you act under pressure, how you make decisions, or how you interact with others. These patterns often happen without you consciously choosing them.
AI Instance
A personal AI companion designed specifically for you, trained on your unique ways of thinking and your values. Not a generic AI, but one that understands and complements your specific consciousness.
Somatic
Related to the body. Somatic awareness means noticing what your body feels - like tension in your shoulders, butterflies in your stomach, or energy in your chest.
